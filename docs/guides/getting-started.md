# Getting Started with PM Simulator

Welcome to PM Simulator - the AI-powered product management training platform that helps you validate ideas through realistic market simulations.

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### 1. Create Your Account

Visit [https://pm-simulator.com](https://pm-simulator.com) and sign up with your email, or use the demo account:
- **Email**: `demo@example.com`
- **Password**: `demo123`

### 2. Start Your First Simulation

1. Click **"New Simulation"** on the dashboard
2. Choose an **Industry Template** (SaaS, FinTech, Health, E-commerce, or AI/ML)
3. Enter your **Product Brief** - describe what you're building and for whom
4. Click **"Create Simulation"**

### 3. Build Your Personas (Optional but Recommended)

Before running the simulation, customize your target audience:

1. Navigate to the **Personas** tab
2. Click **"Add Persona"**
3. Define:
   - **Name & Role**: e.g., "Sarah - CTO at mid-size company"
   - **Pain Level**: How much they need this (1-5)
   - **Tech Savviness**: How comfortable with technology (1-5)
   - **Purchase Urgency**: How quickly they might buy (1-5)

You can create up to 5 personas. Skip this to use AI-generated personas.

### 4. Refine Your Brief with Structured Fields

Use the **Structured Framework** to strengthen your brief:

| Section | Question to Answer |
|---------|---------------------|
| **What** | What exactly are you building? |
| **Who** | Who is your target user? |
| **Why** | Why do they need this? |
| **Impact** | What success looks like? |
| **When** | Timeline for launch? |

Look for the lightbulb icon for contextual hints from product classics like "Inspired" and "The Mom Test".

### 5. Run the Simulation

Choose your simulation mode:

**Rule-Based Mode** (Recommended for first-timers)
- 1,000 autonomous AI agents
- 5-minute runtime
- Statistical metrics: conversion rate, NPS, satisfaction

**LLM Mode** (For deep insights)
- 50 debating agents
- ~15 minutes
- Qualitative debates and detailed insights

Click **"Run Simulation"** and watch real-time progress!

### 6. Analyze Results

After completion, explore:

- **Dashboard Metrics**: Conversion rate, churn, NPS, satisfaction
- **Agent Network**: 3D visualization of how agents interacted
- **Insights**: AI-generated takeaways and recommendations
- **Agent Timeline**: See how individual agents moved through awareness → signup → active → engaged/churned

### 7. Export Your Report

Share results with stakeholders:

1. Click **"Export"** on the results page
2. Choose format: PDF, Word Doc, Notion, or Google Docs
3. Select sections to include
4. Download or share directly

## Key Features

### Persona Builder
Create detailed user profiles with behavioral traits, demographics, and pain points. The simulation adapts to your specific personas.

### Hint System
Get contextual advice from top product management books while you work. Click the lightbulb to see relevant guidance.

### Change Log
Track every edit to your simulation brief. See what changed, when, and why. Revert to previous versions anytime.

### 3D Agent Visualization
Watch your virtual market come alive with an interactive 3D network showing how agents discover, adopt, and discuss your product.

## Tips for Best Results

### Writing Great Briefs

1. **Be specific**: "Project management tool for remote engineering teams" beats "a new app"
2. **Define the problem**: Clearly state the pain point you're solving
3. **Know your audience**: Use the persona builder to define exactly who you're targeting
4. **Set realistic scope**: MVP-focused briefs get more accurate results

### Interpreting Results

- **Conversion Rate**: 2-5% is typical for B2B SaaS
- **NPS**: 50+ is excellent, 30+ is good, below 30 needs work
- **Satisfaction**: 4.0+ indicates strong product-market fit signals

### Iterating Based on Feedback

Use the simulation results to:
1. Adjust your target audience (modify personas)
2. Refine your value proposition (update structured fields)
3. Test different positioning (run multiple simulations)
